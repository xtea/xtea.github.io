❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What are Telegram channels?
Telegram channels are one-way broadcast tools where admins publish content to unlimited subscribers. Unlike groups, only admins can post. Channels are ideal for news, updates, announcements, and content distribution.
How do I find the best Telegram channels?
Use XTEA to search over 50,000 indexed Telegram channels by keyword or category. Browse popular categories like News, Crypto, Tech, Education, and Entertainment to discover top channels.
How do I join a Telegram channel?
Simply search for a channel on XTEA, click the result, and it will open directly in Telegram where you can tap "Join" to subscribe. No registration or payment required.
What are the most popular Telegram channel categories?
The most popular categories include Crypto & Finance, News & Media, Technology, Entertainment, Education, Gaming, and Sports. XTEA indexes channels across all these categories and more.
What's the difference between channels and groups?
Channels are one-way broadcasts — only admins post, subscribers read. Groups are interactive chat rooms where all members can send messages. Channels are best for following content creators and news, while groups are better for discussions.
